Privacy Policy

Cellshift | Effective Date: May 14, 2026 | Last Updated: May 14, 2026

Cellshift ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Excel transformation service.

1. Information We Collect

Files You Upload: When you upload Excel or CSV files for transformation, we process them in real-time within your browser session. We do NOT store, retain, or transmit your uploaded files to any external server. All file processing occurs during your active session and files are permanently discarded when your session ends.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage analytics including: pages visited, features used, transformation types selected, session duration, and general geographic region (country/province level only). We do NOT collect IP addresses or device fingerprints.

Account Information (Pro/Business tiers): If you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and payment information. Payment processing is handled by Stripe and we never store your full credit card number.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Cellshift transformation service
  • To communicate with you about your account or support requests
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our product
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your uploaded files are processed entirely in-memory during your session and are never written to permanent storage. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3) for all data in transit. Our infrastructure is hosted in Canada where possible to comply with Canadian data residency preferences.

4. PIPEDA Compliance

As a Canadian company, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@cellshift.io.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Streamlit Cloud — Application hosting (Snowflake Inc., USA)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (for paid tiers only)
  • Plausible Analytics — Privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics (EU-based)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Cookies

Cellshift uses only essential cookies required for the application to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party marketing cookies. Our analytics tool (Plausible) is cookie-free.

7. Data Retention

Uploaded files: Deleted immediately after session ends (not stored). Account data: Retained while your account is active. Usage analytics: Retained in anonymized form for up to 24 months. You may request deletion of your account data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Cellshift is not intended for use by individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
